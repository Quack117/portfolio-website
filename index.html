Proud Sphynx Cat Father

Two years ago, my wife and I had a discussion about how badly she wanted a dog. After evaluating our situation, we came to the conclusion that we could not properly take care of a dog and ourselves at the same time. Next we talked about getting a cat. "They can take care of themselves", she said! "That's a better idea, but I'm allergic", I replied. That didn't stop my wife. "What about a sphynx cat?" Two months later, we our sphynx cat Eros, and a few months later, we adopted Psyche. I'm still allergic to Sphynx cats, but weekly bathing stops the allergens from building. Sphynx cats are the best!
